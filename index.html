Ch. 4 · The Stacks

A library where the due date is optional

Every shelf in The Study is live: borrow any book with a house library card, or buy it outright and take it home for good. Finish it in a wingback with a cortado, or don't finish it at all — renewals are free with any drink.

New and used, fiction and field guides, plus a rotating shelf picked by the baristas. Trade a finished book back in for credit toward your next pour.
